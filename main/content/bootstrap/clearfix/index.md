Quickly and easily clear floated content within a container by wrapping the content inside the `bootstrap/clearfix` shortcode.

<!--more-->

## Syntax

```markdown
{{%/* bs/clearfix */%}}
FLOATING CONTENT.
{{%/* /bs/clearfix */%}}
```
